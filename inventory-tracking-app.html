Why This Matters

Business Impact: By designing and implementing a comprehensive inventory tracking system using Power App, SharePoint list, Excel database, and custom forms, we transformed manual tracking into an automated, real-time visibility solution. The UI/UX design ensured staff adoption and system usability.

Strategic Value: Power BI dashboards and waste tracking reports provided actionable insights for space planning and quality control. This end-to-end solution—from system design to staff training—created a sustainable, scalable inventory management framework that directly improved operational efficiency and reduced waste.
